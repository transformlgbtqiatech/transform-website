***Note:** There is limited information about how intersex trans persons in India and the larger South Asian region experience violence. Furthermore, the erroneous conflation between being intersex and transgender results in distorted and incomplete information on the violence agaisnt the intersection of these two identities. If you come across any resources, [let us know.](https://transformsouthasia.com/contact-submit)*

* International Commission of Jurists’ [article](https://www.icj.org/refugee-status-claims-based-on-sexual-orientation-and-gender-identity-icj-practitioners-guide-n-11-launched/) on how refugee status is applied for on the basis of sexual orientation and gender identity, including being intersex, to flee fatal violence.
* UNHR’s [report](https://www.ohchr.org/sites/default/files/Documents/Issues/Discrimination/LGBT/BackgroundNoteHumanRightsViolationsagainstIntersexPeople.pdf) on ‘Human Rights Violations Against Intersex People’ documenting violence against intersex persons. 
* Centre for American Progress’ [research](https://www.americanprogress.org/article/key-issues-facing-people-intersex-traits/) on ‘Key Issues Facing People With Intersex Traits’ detailing the different challenges and violations faced by intersex persons in different spheres of their life.
* For more information on the current legal developments regarding the medically unnecessary procedures on intersex children, read Human Rights Watch’s [article](https://www.hrw.org/news/2019/08/29/indian-state-bans-unnecessary-surgery-intersex-children#:~:text=The%20order%20bans%20genital%20surgeries,continue%20performing%20medically%20unnecessary%20operations.), ‘Indian State Bans Unnecessary Surgery on Intersex Children’; and NBC News’ [article](https://www.nbcnews.com/nbc-out/out-news/greece-bans-sex-normalizing-surgeries-intersex-babies-rcna39174), ‘Greece bans ‘sex-normalising’ surgeries on intersex babies’.
